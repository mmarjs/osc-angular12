Web beacons or similar technologies may be used for a number of purposes, including, without limitation, to count visitors to our Service, to monitor how Ocean Service Users navigate the Service, to count how many emails that were sent were actually opened, or to count how many particular articles or links were actually viewed.

An Embedded Script is programming code that is designed to collect information about your interactions with the Service, such as the links you click on.
